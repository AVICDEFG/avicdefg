About Me

Get to know the person behind the work

Hello, I'm Marc Josue C. Bitabara

I'm a passionate Graphic Designer with a background in Visual Communication and Branding. Currently pursuing freelance opportunities and collaborations with a focus on brand identity design and digital illustration.

My journey started with a fascination for visual storytelling in high school, and has led me to develop expertise in logo design, typography, and user-centric visual solutions. I'm driven by a love for aesthetics that communicate meaning, and always eager to take on new challenges that push creative boundaries.

When I'm not working on design projects, you can find me Table Tennis club, exploring art museums, or hiking through nature trails. I believe in the power of thoughtful design to inspire and influence, and strive to create work that resonates with authenticity and purpose.
